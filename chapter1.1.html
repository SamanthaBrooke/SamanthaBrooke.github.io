The Death of Paper
Chapter One

Record collectors will do just about anything to score a long-sought-after album. In Vinyl Freak, music writer, curator, and collector John Corbett reflects on his love affair with vinyl. Discussing more than 200 rare and out-of-print LPs. Vinyl Freak is composed in part of Corbett's long-running DownBeat magazine column of the same name, which was devoted to records that had not appeared on CD.

John Corbetts' novel "Vinyl Freak- Love Letters to a Dying Medium."

Down Beat is an American magazine devoted to celebrating "jazz, blues and beyond."

In April 1979, the magazine went to a monthly schedule for the first time since 1939. in 2010 Down Beat released their first copy of a digital magazine, available to purchase and download onilne.
