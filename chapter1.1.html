Down Beat is an American magazine devoted to "jazz, blues and beyond", the last word indicating its expansion beyond the jazz realm which it covered exclusively in previous years.

Whilst the magazing is still published and widely distributed, the magazine takes advantage of the online capacity to publish, and each issue is now available to download online.
